Aren't you the curious one?

Well, since you are here I'd like to tell you how and why this site was made.

First the why. I wanted to have design a website for myself but I wanted it to be themed. And since I love how comics are laid out, I decided to make this site look like a comics page.

This website was created using JADE and SASS, and not using traditional HTML and CSS. Also, I used FLEXBOX for structure. I also made use of some CSS techniques and jQuery to make everything work together.

About me

I guess you have figured out that I like comics. Here is a bit more about me..

I was born in Shillong, a beautiful city located in the North-Eastern state of Meghalaya. I spent my younger days playing and roaming in its streets, and then I moved to Bangalore - now known as Bengaluru. But not before I developed a keen interest in design.

When I came to Bangalore, priorities changed and I started working in a MNC. Then I changed a few jobs, till the time I got one where I joined as a designer. Previously all were IT related jobs but once I started designing, I realized that I was working at the wrong places! Soon after that, I learned the skills required to become a competant web designer and in the process I learnt about web development as well.

Now after a few years of learning, I think I can say I make pretty cool websites. I personally like to believe this is one of them :P
