Aren't you the curious one?

Well, since you are here I'd like to tell you how this site was made and why.

How - I made this website using Jade, Sass and jQuery. I used Flexbox for structure. The quote of the day is taken from the Brainy Quote website.

Why - I wanted to design a personal website but I also wanted it to be themed. And since I love comics, I decided to try and make the two come together in this one.

About me

Hi, there. Glad to see you here. Let me introduce myself.

I was born in Shillong. I moved to Bangalore in 2007 and have been staying here since.

I have worked in few IT companies in technical roles but once I started designing, I realized that I was working at all the wrong places! Soon after that, I learned the basic skills required to become a competant web designer and in the process I learnt about web development as well.

During all these, I also completed my BSc-IT and MCA. This has helped me to understand how technology works, which I try to implement on the projects I work on.

I am still learning about more complex technologies, but I think I can now say that I make pretty cool websites. I like to believe this is one of them :P
